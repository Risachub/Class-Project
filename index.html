SIMPLE OMELLETE RECIPE


Learn how to make the perfect, fluffy Omelette. Follow these simple tricks to avoid overcooking and ensure they are soft and tender. Once you learn how to make a basic Omelette, there are so many ways to customize it. Watch the video tutorial below and see how easy it is to master homemade Omelettes.

Preparation Time


  • Total: Approximately 10 Minute

  • Preparation: 5 minutes

  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredient


  • Egg

  • Salt

  • Pepper

  • Butter

  • Cheese

Instructions


  1. Beat the Eggs: in a small bowl, combine 2 eggs and a pinch of salt then beat with a fork until frothy.

  2. Heat the Pan: place a small non-stick pan over medium heat and melt in 1/2 Tbsp of butter. Once butter is melted and bubbly, add beaten eggs and reduce heat to low.

  3. Cook the Omellete: Twith a spatula into the center, allowing the liquid eggs to fill in the space.

  4. Flip: once eggs are nearly set, the omelette slides easily on the skillet and you can get a spatula underneath, flip it over. Turn off the heat.

  5. Fold and serve: add cheese and your favorite toppings, fold the omlette and slide it onto a plate to serve.

  6. Enjoy: Serve Hot
